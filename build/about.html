The Idea

Founded by Adam Grossman and Mirza Ibrahimovic in 2010, bluTonic manages a portfolio composed of clients in all verticals, many of which are Fortune 500 companies. Our main focus is data aggregation and management, targeting intelligence and consumer retention. With decades of online experience, bluTonic is a digital media company that endlessly focuses on technological innovations and revenue enhancement systems. Connecting consumers with brands.

We are a diverse group of dedicated and talented individuals who value integrity and good business ethics. We are supportive and readily available to assist our clients at all hours. The vision of this organization is to continuously innovate and improve the field of internet advertising by focusing on research and development of tools used to optimize consumer targeting.

Our Team

We believe in creating an organization that values and encourages creativity and individual ownership of ideas. Our team focuses on both the bottom-line results and its cultural foundation to help brands achieve their online marketing goals and objectives through strategic planning and execution. We learn and adapt in real-time to exceed expectations and deliver results. Hundreds of the world’s leading brands rely on bluTonic to power their display, video and mobile initiatives.

We require all employees to thrive in a fast paced, high-performance environment. Enthusiasm, quick reaction times, flexibility and timely problem solving are just a few characteristics that describe our staff. We believe in clear and direct communication. Our goal is to shape business decisions, generate customer loyalty and create an inspiring culture for all team members.
